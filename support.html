Baggage

General Information

– Checked Baggage:

For health and safety reasons, each piece of baggage that is checked in must not exceed 32kg. Its total dimensions (width + heights + depth) must not exceed 203 cm. Important Notice: You will be required to re-pack your bag if the weight of a piece of checked baggage exceeds 32kg. Alternatively, you may want to consider sending your baggages as cargo. Please contact us prior to your travel.

– Hand Baggage:

To comply with safety standards, each piece of hand luggage must not exceed 7kg. Its the total dimensions (width + heights + depth) must not exceed 115cm (56 cm x 36 cm x 23 cm, or: 22’’ x 11‘’ x 9’’) Important Notice: If your hand baggage exceeds the quantity, weight and dimensions specified above, the luggage must be checked in.

– Baggage Tips:

• It’s always helpful to personalize your baggage with stickers or tags. You can also tag your baggage with your name and contact information so you can find your baggage more easily after your flight.

• We recommend that you pack your belongings in bags that are designed for travel to avoid damage to your luggage or its contents during transport.

• In order to safeguard your property and prevent any losses, damages or delays, Vietnam Airlines would like to remind you not to pack any fragile, perishable, valuable, or important personal items in your checked baggage.

• Any item that exceeds weight requirements and/or exceeds the maximum size (width + height + depth) mentioned above may be rejected.

• In addition, we recommend that each piece of your checked baggage not exceed 32kg to avoid injury to porters, especially for customers traveling to and from Australia, New Zealand, South Africa, Sri Lanka, and the United Kingdom. This is in accordance with local occupational safety rules.

• The Free Baggage Allowance might vary for different carriers. Please consider this if you are connecting to/from another carrier or if you are taking any Vietnam Airlines code sharing flight that is operated by other airlines. Please refer to the Interline Baggage Rules for more details.

Baggage Allowance

– On Vietnam Airlines flights, you are offered the following free baggage allowance:

    • Business & Premium Class: 30kg checked baggage + two pieces of hand baggage

    • Economy Class: 20kg checked baggage + one piece of hand baggage

– Note:

    • Free baggage allowance for infants (under 2 years old)

    • From/To the Americas: One piece, with a maximum weight of 23kg and sum of dimensions (width + height + depth) not exceeding 115cm (45 in), and one fully collapsible stroller.

    • Other itineraries: One piece, with a maximum weight of 10kg, and one fully collapsible stroller

    • In some cases, the baggage allowances printed on your ticket may be different from above mentioned. Baggage allowances printed on your ticket will be applied

    • For itineraries to/from the Americas, the baggage allowance policy of the first marketing carrier will be applied for the whole route. (The first marketing carrier is the first carrier whose code appears on your ticket)

    • If the journey includes multiple sectors and carriers, baggage allowances may differ between carriers

    • Unless otherwise specified, the maximum weight per piece of baggage is 32kg, and total dimensions may not exceed 203cm. If you need to bring overweight and/or oversized baggage, please kindly contact Vietnam Airlines official agents or Vietnam Airlines offices for further information

Check In

Online Checkin

    “Online boarding passes” are issued after completion of web/mobile application check-in process. They can be self-printed by passengers or received via email/e-device. Front-line staffs at airport check-in counter will check “Online boarding passes” and stamp on them or change to traditional boarding passes.

    Online check-in counters are airport check-in counters for passengers who complete online check-in process, to verify their online boarding passes. To locate the online check-in counters, passengers can follow airports’ guidance or contact airports’ staffs for assistance.

Airport Check In

    Important notes:

    • If flight schedule is changed, check-in time will be adjusted accordingly.

    • Check-in timing also depends on each different individual airport and carrier.

    • Passengers need to arrive at the airport early to have enough time for check-in, security check, and immigration procedures.

    • Depending on your fare conditions, a failure to check-in on time may also result in:

    • Making your ticket invalid for travel or becoming non-refund in case there are restrictions on changes. You will need to make a new booking and purchase a new ticket.

    • Paying change fee, and/or fares difference for the new applicable fare.

    • Due to potential traffic delays, check-in time and security screening, you are recommended to be at the airport at least 60 minutes before departure of domestic flights and 120 minutes before departure of international flights. The handing of stand by passengers bases on the method “first comes, first serve” in each priority level.

Special Services

Passengers With Disabilites

    Vietnam Airlines always provides passengers with disabilities our warmest assistance. Vietnam Airlines offers the following Mobility Aid services: • Blind Passenger Service – free of charge; • Deaf Passenger Service – free of charge; • Wheelchair Service – free of charge; • Service dog accompanying disabilities – free of charge; • Disabilities passenger’s wheelchair – free of charge; • Accompanying cabin crew service – fee applied;(except on flights to/from the US). • Please inform our branch offices of any special assistance while travelling with us. Important Note: Vietnam Airlines accepts for carriage accompanied/unaccompanied blind, deaf or with problems walking if he/she is otherwise healthy and does not require medical clearance. Passengers requiring medical clearance see details here.

Pregnant Passengers

    Vietnam Airlines will bring pregnant passengers the best comfort and special assistance. For safety concerns, pregnant passengers are advised to inform our staff about your pregnancy status at reservation. In general, it is safe to fly during pregnancy. However, Vietnam Airlines advise all pregnant passengers to consult their doctor before planning a trip. • Consult your physician in advance to see if it is safe for you to fly. • Consult with your doctor if you have any systems such as bleeding or morning sickness, or if you have any complications including threatened miscarriage, anemia, or toxemia of pregnancy during pregnancy. • Reduce stress as much as possible. • The best time to travel during pregnancy is the stable period between 12 and 28 weeks.

Forms Of Identification

Passengers with non-Vietnamese nationalities travelling on Vietnam domestic flights submit one of the following identification forms: National Passport; Travel Document, Loose - leaf Visa, permanent residence card; temporary residence card issued by Vietnam authority. In case passport loss, a diplomatic note from the diplomatic mission or consulate of the host country bearing the nationality or official letter of the foreign affairs or police office of the locality where the loss of personal identity and Passport with portrait pictures and stamp is required. Diplomatic note is valid within 30 days since the date of confirmation.

Identity cards issued by diplomatic agencies to members of diplomatic missions, consular agencies and representatives of international organizations such as: diplomatic identity cards, consular identity cards, official identity cards, honorary consul identity cards, and general identity cards.

Passengers with Vietnamese nationality from 14 years of age travelling on Vietnam domestic flights submit one of the following identification forms:

Passport or Travel Document; Loose - leaf Visa, permanent residence card; temporary residence card, Identity card; Military Identity card; National Assembly membership card; Communist Party of Vietnam membership card; Press Identity card; Driving licence; Aviation Security control card; National Security Aviation Committee card; Airlines Identity card; Certifying personal identification form by local police or authorities about passenger ‘s permanent residence or temporary residence; Certifying form by authorities about passenger ‘s sentenced completion.

Above certifying forms must be attached with stamped portrait picture and shall be valid within 30 days since the date of confirmation.

Passengers with Vietnamese nationality under 14 years of age travelling on Vietnam domestic flights submit one of the following identification forms:

Passport;

Birth Certificate;

Birth Certifying Form (infants under one (01) month old);

Certifying form by a social organization for children under the patronage of that organisation. This certifying form shall be valid within 6 months since the date of confirmation.

Passengers’ travel documents as referred above shall meet the following conditions:

Travel documents are still valid;

Birth Certificate and Birth Certifying Form must be the original ones or copy one certified by notarial office with legal status.
